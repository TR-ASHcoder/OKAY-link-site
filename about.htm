why?

You might be asking, "Why?" To that, I say... elaborate? Instead, I’m going to assume you’re wondering why I even bothered making this site. Well, in my opinion, services like Linktree are a waste of money when I know I can create something similar myself in a day—which is exactly what I did! Plus, it’s a fun way to showcase all my social media profiles, projects, and HTML/CSS skills. It’s a personal project that lets me combine my love for technology with practical applications.
